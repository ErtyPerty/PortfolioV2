Navbars

Burger!

The burger navigation type is the 3 lines you see. They are most commonly used to show that there is a hidden menu that you can access by clicking it. They are most commonly used to navigate to different pages in a dropdown box.

Breadcrumbs

Breadcrumbs are a type of navigation, simillar to a file layout. Infact, you have already used a breadcrumb. It's how you got here. The navigation bar at the top is an example of the breadcrumbs navigation styles
